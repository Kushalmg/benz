Mercedes Ben'z

Mercedes-Benz is a world-renowned German luxury automobile manufacturer that is part of the Daimler AG group. It is known for producing high-end vehicles, including luxury cars, trucks, buses, and coaches. The brand is synonymous with quality, performance, and innovation in the automotive industry.Founded: The roots of Mercedes-Benz trace back to the late 19th century. The company emerged from the vision of two pioneers: Karl Benz: In 1886, he created the first practical automobile, the Benz Patent Motorwagen, often considered the first true car.Gottlieb Daimler and Wilhelm Maybach: They created an early high-speed engine and also developed one of the first automobiles.Brand Formation: Mercedes-Benz, as a brand, was officially formed in 1926 when the two companies, Benz & Cie. and Daimler-Motoren-Gesellschaft (DMG), merged.
